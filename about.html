Our Divine Origin Story

"We started with nothing but a grill, a dream, and a whole lot of beef."

In the Beginning, There Was Hunger

It all started on a fateful Tuesday in 2019 when Daniel Lupase had what can only be described as a burger epiphany. After yet another disappointing fast-food experience that left his soul as empty as his wallet, Daniel looked up at the sky and made a sacred vow: "There has to be a better way to burger."

That very night, Daniel claims he had a dream where a chorus of angels (who looked suspiciously like his grandmother) taught him the secret to the perfect burger. When he woke up, he found a mysterious recipe scribbled on his nightstand in what appeared to be golden ink. Skeptics say it was just a regular pen, but Daniel knows divine intervention when he sees it.

Armed with this heavenly knowledge and a secondhand grill he named "Gabriel," Daniel began his mission to bring burger salvation to the masses. What started as weekend cookouts for friends quickly became a neighborhood phenomenon, with people lining up for blocks just to taste what they called "miracle burgers."

What Makes Us Heaven-Sent

Our grill masters undergo a rigorous training program that some say borders on the mystical. They learn to read the burger's aura, to understand the precise moment when a patty reaches enlightenment, and to channel their inner zen while flipping. The result? Burgers that are seared to perfection with the precision of a Swiss watch and the soul of a poet.
